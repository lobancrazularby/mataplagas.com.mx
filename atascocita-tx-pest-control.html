How To Control Mosquitoes In Atascocita, TX

Thanks to all the warm weather, damp summers, and high humidity levels found in Atascocita, mosquitoes are a serious problem for homeowners at all times of the year. Neighborhoods near Lake Houston and other water-heavy areas are forced to deal with high volumes of mosquitoes every summer, sometimes completely without relief.

Mosquitoes are both dangerous and unwelcome pests in Texas and should be discouraged from entering every last Atascocita yard. To combat their rising numbers and decrease your risks for disease, homeowners in Atascocita are encouraged to try at least one of the following prevention steps:

  • Keep the grass cut low in the yard. Shorter grass leads to less water retention,    which dries up areas that mosquitoes could be living and breeding in.
  • Trim all tree branches which may be growing too close to the home.
  • Drain as many areas of standing water as possible. Non-movable water sources should be treated with a mosquito larvicide as soon as possible.

Atascocita's Solution To Termite Control Problems

Termites are increasingly dangerous pests to Atascocita, Texas households, and rightfully so. Control their spread around your property with these prevention tips: 

  • Keep household moisture levels to a manageable level with the purchase of a dehumidifier.

  • Consider removing your landscaping mulch and replacing it with peat stone or some form of gravel.
