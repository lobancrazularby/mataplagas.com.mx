What Every Mission Resident Ought To Know About Termite Control

Termites are some of the most common home-invaders in the Mission area, but they’re also some of the most destructive. It only takes 18 to 24 months for termites to do an average of $3,000’ worth of damage on your property, and many homeowners allow this to happen by not recognizing the signs of an infestation. 

These signs include:

  • Mud tubes along foundation walls.
  • Shed wings of swarmer termites.
  • Clicking noises within the walls.
  • Visible damage to the exposed wood.
  • Tightening around doors and windows.

Unfortunately, it can be very difficult to spot these signs yourself, which is why White Knight Pest Control recommends that every homeowner has a professional termite inspection done. If you do notice these signs, then White Knight Pest Control should be your first call. Trying to get rid of termites yourself will only allow the infestation to linger in other areas. 

If you’re worried about a current or future termite problem, don’t waste another second. One phone call could save you thousands of dollars in the battle against property-damaging termites. Call us today to schedule a professional termite inspection.
